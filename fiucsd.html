FI @ UCSD

FI is a design agency that I am a founding member, leader, and UX mentor of at UC San Diego alongside Shan Prasad and Jose Alvarenga We set out to answer a problem that we observed in the Design community at UC San Diego: students lacking projects, industry connections, and real world design experience that isn't offered in a traditional classroom setting. This is where Fi comes in!

We are now a growing team of student researchers and designers who deliver innovative, user-centerd solutions to clients of all sizes, with all challenges.

I currenty work as the innovation director as well as providing guidance and mentorship to our designers.
