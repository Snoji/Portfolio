FI @UCSD

Check out more photos here

Fi @UCSD is a UX design agency, aimed at bridging the gap between students and clients within and outsie UC San Diego to collaborate on projects that utilize design thinking.

I am a founding member, leader, and UX mentor of Fi alongside Shan Prasad and Jose Alvarenga We set out to answer a problem that we found in the Design community at UC San Diego: students lack industry-relevant experience to hone their skills, collaborate with others, and grow confidence in their roles in design. This is where Fi comes in!

We are a growing team of student researchers and designers who deliver innovative, user-centerd solutions to clients of all sizes, with all challenges.

I work as the innovation director and provide design guidance and mentorship to our agents.

Come learn more at fi-ucsd.com
