Ripple

About

My team and I were tasked to create an application, in 5 weeks, that catered to the most overlooked age group - people 45 years and older. Ripple attempts to answer this by providing them with a place to keep track off their bucket list while finding new hobbies and fostering new connections along the way.​​​​​​​

Key Skills

Rapid Ideation • Experience Design • Prototyping • Branding

Problem

As people grow older, they want to start checking things off of their bucket list. However, it is hard to find ways to accomplish these tasks and keep track of the rest. Along with this, old age brings about problems such as loneliness, lack of social interaction, and in extreme cases, depression.
