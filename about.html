About Me

Hello! My name is Franklin! I'm a fourth year student at UC San Diego,
pursing a degree in Matematics with a focus in Applied Science and a
minor in Design.

I've worked on many projects varying in product design, research,
web development, and much more. Currently I am working on a couple
of personal projects in the scope of app development and product design.

I love observation! The simple act of sitting and observing how people
interact with their enviornment and seeing what things they desire but
don't notice sparks joy and many ideas within me.

In my free time, I enjoy learning about new technology, gaming
(currently Apex Legends), and choreographing to whatever song is stuck
in my head at the moment (something Billie Eilish).

Thanks for reading ~ and feel free to drop a line. Stay Curious!
